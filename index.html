The Sovereign

Prepare yourself for a royal treat. The Sovereign is a welcoming and lively neighbourhood cafe dedicated to the absolute rule of exceptional coffee. It's place where regulars are greeted by name, locals meet to hear the news and connoisseurs flock to sample the best shots pulled this side of the Bridge of Sighs.

The Sovereign was founded by childhood friends Rosario Salvi and Gianfranco Voce. Born and raised in Davenport Village, they set out to create a coffee destination and a community hub. Since opening their doors in 2012, they've been joined by head chef Anthony Menna and now offer brunch, house-made snacks, and baked goods.

With their love of hosting and devotion to grinding only the most delectable beans, Salvi, Voce and Menna invite you to drop by and find out why The Sovereign rules.
